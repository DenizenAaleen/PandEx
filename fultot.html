Fultot

Undead that have broken free of control from the Corrusaur and/or their soul-bind.
Undead
Trait Cost
Type Undead 16
No Con Score. Use Cha Score in place of Con for calculating hitpoints, foritude saves, and any special ability that relies on Con, such as a breath weapon
Darkvision 60ft
Immune to Mind-affecting effects, bleed damage, death effects disease, paralysis, poison, sleep effects, stunning and physical ability score damage
Not subject to nonlethal damage, ability drain, energy drain, exhaustion or fatigue effects
Immune to effects that require a Fort save unless they also work on objects or is harmless
Are not at risk of death from massive damage, but immediately are destroyed upon reaching 0 hitpoints
Are unaffected by raise dead and reincarnate spells and abilities, but resurrection and true resurrection will revert them into the living creature they were before they became undead
Do not need to breathe, eat, or sleep, but may choose to inorder to gain a beneficial effect, such as drinking a potion
Harmed by positive energy and healed by negative energy
Special Note Fast healing still works as normal
