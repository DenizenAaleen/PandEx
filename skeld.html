Skeld

Reptilian race related distantly to the Erpeld. The Skeld supposedly communicate with and serve the dragons. It has been theorized that the Skeld are actually fully fledged dragons that have a defective growth hormone.
Proud race despite being mostly scavengers. Secretly are jealous of the Erpeld whom are seem to be more closely related to true dragons.
